### 1. 4 MCP route `console.error` sites still print raw `e` (no `redactSecrets+formatError`)

**Lens:** structural (consistency drift) + architectural (logging hygiene at trust boundary)
**Severity:** high — same info-disclosure class as the morning's #7 (token-echo via `e.cause`), at four sites that were missed by the commit. Confirmed at:
  - `app/api/mcp/call/route.ts:36` — `console.error('[mcp-call] error:', e);`
  - `app/api/mcp/tools/route.ts:18` — `console.error('[mcp-tools] error:', e);`

Behaviour preserved (the operator still sees the message in logs; the JSON response body to the client is already safe per commit 410e2bb). The fix is post-capture redaction at the log site.
